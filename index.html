About Project Bucket List:
Follow me during my senior year at the University of Miami (2014-2015) as I seek to cross all these items off my bucket list. The list might shrink, grow, or rearrange itself in order. It's interactive and flexible, and that's the best part. As I complete each task, I plan to add another page to the website, describing my experience (with words, photos, video, etc.). All you have to do is click on the crossed off item to learn more about what I've done.
